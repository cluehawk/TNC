MANUAL PLAYTEST CHECKLIST
=========================

1. Start the program and confirm the 2x2 hub appears near the center.
2. Move each prisoner one step and confirm new connected tiles generate.
3. Leave an area behind and confirm unlit unoccupied tiles vanish.
4. Find or force a crumbling tile. Enter it, then move away and confirm it becomes a pit.
5. Stay on an armed crumbling tile and confirm the prisoner begins falling.
6. On the falling prisoner's next turn, try a valid and an invalid re-entry edge.
7. Move into line of sight of a monster and confirm lights-out plus draw loss.
8. Move a lit prisoner adjacent to a lights-out prisoner and confirm relight.
9. Spend nerve for an extra action and confirm the same prisoner acts again.
10. Continue until draws reach zero and confirm final flickers begin.
11. Spend nerve to cancel a final flicker, then skip the spend and confirm a lit tile is removed.
12. Gather keys and stack all four keyed prisoners on a gate to confirm victory.
